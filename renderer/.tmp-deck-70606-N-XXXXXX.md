# MoveIt × FastFreight  
## Joint‑Venture Proposal for a Shared Last‑Mile Network  
**Partnership Committee** – *Collaborative & Strategic*  

---

# Why a Partnership Now?  

- **Growing demand:** Same‑day & hyperlocal deliveries up 38% YoY (2024)  
- **Capacity strain:** Both firms hitting 85‑90% utilization in core metros  
- **Cost pressure:** Fuel, labor, and urban‑access fees rising >12% p.a.  
- **Opportunity:** Pool resources to unlock economies of scale & network effects  

> *Alone we can expand; together we can reshape the market.*

---

# Strategic Fit  

| Dimension | MoveIt | FastFreight | Combined Advantage |
|-----------|--------|------------|--------------------|
| **Geographic footprint** | Strong in NE & Midwest | Dominant in SE & West Coast | Nationwide coverage with minimal overlap |
| **Technology stack** | AI‑driven route optimizer | Real‑time fleet telematics | Integrated platform > sum of parts |
| **Customer base** | Retail & e‑commerce SMBs | Enterprise shippers & 3PLs | Cross‑sell potential ↑ 25% |
| **Culture** | Entrepreneurial, agile | Process‑focused, reliable | Balanced innovation & execution |

---

# Proposed Operating Model  

- **Legal entity:** 50/50 JV (NewCo) – governance via equal‑weight board  
- **Shared assets:**  
  - Consolidated micro‑fulfillment hubs (30 pilot sites)  
  - Joint fleet of electric vans (leased via JV)  
- **Technology:** Single API layer exposing MoveIt optimizer + FastFreight telematics  
- **Commercial:**  
  - MoveIt retains front‑end SMB sales; FastFreight handles enterprise contracts  
  - Revenue‑share model: 60% to JV, 40% allocated to parent per usage
