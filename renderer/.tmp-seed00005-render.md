# Risk discipline stays non-negotiable

- Advance only projects at Phase II interconnection or better
- Lock 70% of EPC pricing before final investment decision
- Cap open merchant exposure at 20% by asset
- Review permitting, offtake, and counterparty flags monthly

# Board decisions for this quarter

- Approve the $1.2B deployment envelope
- Authorize selective asset recycling above 15% gross MOIC
- Endorse an 8% single-market concentration cap
- Hold quarterly gate reviews on NTP, COD, and yield

### The ask is disciplined scaling, not growth at any cost.
