# Slide 1 – Executive Summary
## H1 2024 Performance Snapshot
- **Revenue**: $212.3 M (+5.8% YoY) – driven by 3% comparable sales growth and net new openings
- **EBITDA**: $27.6 M (+4.2% YoY) – margin 13.0% (flat vs. 2023 H)
- **Free Cash Flow**: $18.1 M (+6.1% YoY) – conversion 65.6% of EBITDA
- **Store Network**: 184 locations ( +12 net YoY )
- **Capital Discipline**: CapEx $9.4 M (maintenance 55%, growth 45%)

*Key question: Are we expanding profitably and with discipline?*  
*Answer preview: Yes – growth funded by operating cash, returns above hurdle, and guardrails in place.*

|--------|--------|--------|-------|
| Gross openings | 10 | 7 | +3 |

| Net new stores | **+8** | **+6** | +2 |

# Slide 5 – Capital Allocation & Returns
- **CapEx breakdown (H1 2024)**
  - Maintenance & refurbishment: $5.2 M (55%)
  - New‑store build‑out: $4.2 M (45%)
- **Return on Invested Capital (ROIC)**
  - Store‑level ROIC (new stores, 12‑month): 12.4%
  - Portfolio‑level ROIC: 10.9% (company WACC ≈ 8.0%)
- **Cash conversion**
  - FCF / EBITDA: 65.6%
  - Debt repayment (net): $3.1 M (leverage ↓ to 2.1x EBITDA)

*Conclusion:* Capital is generating returns comfortably above the cost of cash, supporting sustainable expansion.
