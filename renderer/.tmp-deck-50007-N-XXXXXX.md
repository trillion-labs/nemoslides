# H2 2024 Priorities & Guardrails

**Strategic Priorities**
1. **Drive Comparable‑Store Sales** – Target +5% SSSG via menu innovation, localized digital loyalty, and speed‑of‑service improvements.  
2. **Labor Efficiency** – Reduce labor cost to ≤ 17.5% of revenue through AI‑driven scheduling and cross‑training.  
3. **Pipeline Execution** – Open the remaining 8 stores with capex capped at $1.3 M/store; maintain ≥ 15% store‑level contribution.  
4. **Supply‑Chain Optimization** – Achieve 50 bps COGS reduction via consolidated purchasing and waste‑reduction initiatives.  
5. **Balance Sheet Strength** – Keep Debt/EBITDA ≤ 2.8× and maintain free cash flow conversion ≥ 40%.

**Guardrails (to be monitored monthly)**
- Capex ≤ 7% of revenue.  
- New‑store IRR ≥ 12% (post‑open).  
- SG&A ≤ 22% of revenue.  
- Same‑store sales growth ≥ 3% (floor).  
- No lease exceeding 10 years without CFO sign‑off.

*These priorities and guardrails ensure that H2 growth remains profitable, measurable, and aligned with the private‑equity mandate of disciplined value creation.*
