# Lessons Learned

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-8 mt-2">

<div>

### ✅ What Worked
- **LinkedIn + Google Search combo** drove 73% of SQLs and 79% of pipeline
- **Gated webinar content** produced highest-quality leads despite higher CPL
- **Tight lead routing** (< 5 min SLA) improved MQL→SQL on inbound by 18% vs. Q2
- **Audience targeting refinement** (ICP narrowed to VP+ / 200–5K FTE) reduced wasted spend by ~14%

</div>

<div>

### ⚠️ What Needs Fixing
- **Content Synd. quality gap** — 86% of leads never convert to SQL; vendor mix needs overhaul
- **SDR outbound efficiency** — 8 SQLs from 281 leads; messaging and ICP alignment off
- **SQL-to-Opp rate slipped** to 39% (target 45%) — sales enablement handoff needs tightening
- **Lead→SQL cycle time** +5 days vs. target — nurture sequences require optimization

</div>

</div>

<div class="mt-6 p-4 bg-gray-100 dark:bg-gray-800 rounded text-sm">
  <strong>Meta-Lesson:</strong> We are generating enough leads. The bottleneck is <em>depth of conversion</em>, not <em>top-of-funnel volume</em>. Scaling without quality fixes will widen the gap.
</div>

# Scaling Opportunities

### Where We Double Down vs. Restructure

| Lever | Action | Expected Impact | Confidence |
|---|---|---|:---:|
| Increase LinkedIn budget +35% | More of highest-ROAS channel | +112 SQLs, +$2.4M pipe | High |
| Scale Google Search | Expand to adjacent keywords (data governance, cloud analytics) | +65 SQLs, +$1.4M pipe | High |
| Double webinar cadence | 2 webinars/month (currently 1) | +38 SQLs, +$810K pipe | Medium-High |
| Restructure Content Syndication | Drop 2 low-quality vendors, test 1 premium network | CPL rises but SQL rate ↑ to 22% | Medium |
| Overhaul SDR outbound | New messaging framework + tighter ICP list | SQL rate ↑ to 25%, fewer leads but better | Medium |
| Implement lead scoring v2 | Add intent + engagement signals; route only high-score to sales | Reduce cycle time 3–5 days | Medium-High |

<div class="mt-4 text-sm">
  <strong>Projected Q4 outcome with all levers:</strong> ~260 additional SQLs, ~$4.9M incremental pipeline, blended CPL ~$168, blended ROAS ~9.2x
</div>
