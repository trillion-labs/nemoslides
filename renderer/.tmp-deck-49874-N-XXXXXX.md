# Slide 2 – Risk Assessment Methodology  

- **Framework**: COSO ERM + ISA 315 (Risk Assessment)  
- **Inputs**:  
  - Prior year audit findings  
  - Regulatory updates (Basel IV, CFPB)  
  - Internal control testing results (Q1‑Q2 2024)  
  - Emerging macro‑economic stressors (inflation, interest‑rate volatility)  
- **Scoring**: Likelihood × Impact (1‑5) → Risk Rating (Low, Medium, High, Critical)  
- **Output**: Top‑5 material risks plotted on heat map

# Slide 4 – Near‑Term Mitigation Actions (Q3‑Q4 2024)  

- **R1 – Credit Quality**  
  - Enhance sector‑limit monitoring; introduce stress‑test scenarios for CRE & leveraged finance (by 15 Sep)  
  - Deploy revised PD/LGD models; quarterly portfolio review with Board Risk Committee  

- **R2 – Cyber Security**  
  - Complete penetration test & red‑team exercise (by 30 Sep)  
  - Implement zero‑trust network segmentation for payment‑switch infrastructure (by 31 Oct)  

- **R3 – Regulatory Compliance**  
  - Update AML transaction‑monitoring rules; roll‑out enhanced KYC enhanced due‑diligence for high‑risk clients (by 15 Oct)  
  - Conduct fair‑lending training for all loan‑origination staff (by 31 Oct)  

- **R4 – Interest‑Rate Risk**  
  - Re‑balance ALM book: increase duration of liabilities via floating‑rate deposits (by 15 Nov)  
  - Run monthly VaR & earnings‑at‑risk reporting to ALCO  

- **R5 – Third‑Party Resilience**  
  - Activate vendor‑risk scoring dashboard; require BCPs for top‑5 providers (by 30 Sep)  
  - Conduct tabletop exercise for critical provider outage scenario (by 15 Nov)
