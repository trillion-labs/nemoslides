## Risks & Blockers

1. **Unclear Definition of Ready** – 22 % of stories require clarification mid‑sprint, increasing carry‑over.  
2. **Shared infra bottlenecks** – DB migration windows cause 1.5 sprint delay per quarter.  
3. **Limited automated debt tracking** – manual ticket triage adds ~0.8 sprint overhead per month.

---

## Recommendation

- **Allocate a dedicated 15 % capacity buffer each quarter for tech‑debt reduction** (target: bring debt story points ≤10 % of velocity).  
- **Enforce a stricter Definition of Ready** and adopt a ready‑check checklist to cut mid‑sprint clarification by half.  
- **Invest in automated debt‑identification tooling** (e.g., SonarQube debt plugin) to reduce manual triage effort by 40 %.  
- **Pilot a bi‑weekly infra‑window sync** to smooth DB migration impact and reduce carry‑over.
