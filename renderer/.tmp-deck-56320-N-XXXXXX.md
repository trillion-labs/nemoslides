<!-- Slide 5: Drivers of Over/Under‑Spend -->
# What’s Behind the Numbers?

- **Cost Inflation**  
  - Avg. CPM across paid media ↑ **10 %** YoY (industry benchmark).  
  - CPC for core keywords ↑ **12 %**, impacting Paid Search ROI.  

- **Creative Fatigue**  
  - Creative refresh lagged → **3‑4 pp drop** in conversion rates on Social Paid.  

- **Influencer Mis‑allocation**  
  - Over‑investment in macro‑influencers with diminishing incremental lift.  
  - KPI mis‑alignment: focused on reach vs. conversion.  

- **Planning Rigor**  
  - Media plan lacked granular weekly cadence → overspend in early weeks, under‑utilized late‑week inventory.  

---

<!-- Slide 6: Recommendations for Q3 Mix -->
# Recommended Q3 Adjustments

1. **Re‑allocate Budget**  
   - Shift **$0.4 M** from Influencer budget to Paid Search & Social Paid to capture high‑intent inventory.  

2. **Tighten KPI Alignment**  
   - Move Influencer contracts to **performance‑based (CPA) models** only.  

3. **Creative Refresh Cadence**  
   - Implement **bi‑weekly creative A/B tests**; target **+0.4 pp** conversion uplift.  

4. **Cost‑Control Structures**  
   - Introduce **capped CPM bids** & frequency caps to mitigate cost spikes.  

5. **Incremental Testing**  
   - Pilot a **small‑scale algorithmic bidding test** for Paid Search (target +5 % ROAS).  

*Resulting impact:* Projected **budget compliance at 98‑100 %** and **+0.3× ROI uplift** in Q3.
